About Me

Hello! I'm Karan Thakkar, a dedicated doctoral student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at Johns Hopkins University, Baltimore, USA. My academic quest is deeply rooted in the fields of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Brain-Inspired Computational Models for Audio. Working under the expert guidance of Professor Mounya Elhilali at the Laboratory of Computational Audio Perception, I am at the forefront of researching and developing groundbreaking auditory perception-inspired neural networks.

My journey in this fascinating realm began with a Bachelor of Technology in Electronics and Communication Engineering from the Vellore Institute of Technology, India, where I graduated in June 2020. My undergraduate research, conducted jointly with VIT Vellore and NUS Singapore, delved into deep learning applications in wireless communication and emotion recognition in speech, showcasing my versatility and drive to explore new technological frontiers. This foundation was pivotal in shaping my interest in the dynamic intersection of technology, human experience, and auditory perception.

Before embarking on my doctoral studies, I contributed as a Machine Learning Engineer at DeepSync Technologies, where I specialized in audio data processing and developed scalable text-to-speech systems. In addition to my research, I am actively involved in academic projects and open-source contributions, showcasing my commitment to collaborative learning and knowledge sharing.

Beyond my academic and professional pursuits, I have a profound passion for the arts. In my free time, I indulge in painting and exploring the creative world of music mixing, allowing me to blend my technical expertise with artistic expression.

I invite you to join me on this exciting journey of discovery and innovation as I continue to push the boundaries in the field of electrical and computer engineering and audio perception. If you're interested in my research or have any questions, please feel free to reach out. I am always eager to engage in thoughtful conversations and collaborations!
